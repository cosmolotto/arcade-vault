Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED · MAY 13, 2026

Arcade Vault is a free-to-play game collection. We respect your privacy and collect almost nothing.

Summary: Game scores are stored locally on your device. We don't track you. We don't have your data because we don't collect it.

What stays on your device

  • Your high scores
  • Game progress (levels reached, achievements)
  • UI preferences

All of this lives in browser localStorage on your device. We have no copy.

What we collect

  • Anonymous analytics: page views, which games are played — aggregated, not linked to you
  • MRV wallet address only if you connect a wallet to claim rewards — this is a public on-chain address

What we do not collect

  • No accounts, no logins, no passwords
  • No email addresses
  • No phone numbers
  • No advertising trackers or fingerprinting
  • No personally identifiable information

Rewards and MRV

When you connect a wallet to claim MRV rewards for winning a game, that wallet address is used to credit the reward. No off-chain identity is linked to it.

Children

Arcade Vault is rated for everyone. We do not knowingly collect data from children, because we do not collect data at all.

Data sharing

We have nothing to share. We do not sell, license, or transfer user data to third parties.

Security

All traffic is HTTPS. Game logic runs entirely in your browser.
